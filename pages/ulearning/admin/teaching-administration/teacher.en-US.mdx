# Teacher Management

This section provides instructions for creating, editing, and deleting teacher accounts, as well as guidelines on how to act as a teacher.
![](/img/ulearning/admin/teacher01.png)
## Add Teacher
1. Click the Teachers tab [1].
2. You can filter/search teachers [2].
3. You can add a teacher manually [3].

![](/img/ulearning/admin/teacher02.png)
## Import Teachers in Bulk
1. You can also import teachers in bulk [4]. 

## Edit Teacher
1. To edit a teacher account, click the **Edit** link[5] for the selected account. 

## Delete Teacher
1. To delete a teacher account, click the **Delete** [6] link for the relevant account.

## Act as Teacher

When clicking the Act as Teacher link [7] for a teacher account, you will log in as that teacher and open the teacher’s landing page, and then you can perform all the actions as if you were that teacher. Please refer the ULearning Teacher Guide to see what actions you can perform as a teacher.

To return to your admin account, click the **Back to Admin Center** link.
![](/img/ulearning/admin/teacher03.png)

<Callout type="info" emoji="ℹ️"> 
    Ensure adherence to privacy and data handling policies when managing teacher accounts.
</Callout>
