# Exam Management Guide

Effectively manage exams with the following functionalities.
![](/img/ulearning/admin/exam01.png)

**Accessing the Console**
1. Click the **Exams** tab **[1]**.
   - Utilize the filter/search feature to locate exams **[2]** (searching is not case-sensitive).
   - Open the Exam Management Console by clicking the **View Results** link **[3]** for a specific exam.

## Exam Management Console
![](/img/ulearning/admin/exam02.png)

### Editing Exam Instructions
1. Edit Exam Instructions **[1]** for an exam.
   - These instructions are visible to exam-takers on the exam home screen before starting the exam.

### Other Actions
1. Explore a range of actions within the Exam Management Console:
   - Refer to the ULearning Teacher Guide **[2]** for a comprehensive list of actions available to the exam owner.
