# Overview

This is a quick-start user guide for students. The aim of this guide is to give the ULearning student users the necessary knowledge and skills to use ULearning LMS. By following the steps in this guide, you can quick start your journey to perform the most common LMS tasks as a student.

This guide is organized around the necessary tasks you will perform, step by step accompanied by ULearning platform screenshots.
