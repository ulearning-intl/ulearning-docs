# Viewing/Reading an Announcement

Announcements are used by your teacher to communicate with you about the logistics of your course. Your teacher may use Announcements to:
- Remind you what you need to accomplish to stay on track.
- Point you to internal and external resources that will help you achieve course outcomes.
- Leave a message for the entire class with video or audio comments.
- Celebrate student success and important events that may be of interest to you..

### Step 1
For an announcement, click the **Expand** button to read the whole message.
- Notes: The sign "Read" indicates that you haven't read the full message of this announcement.

![](/img/ulearning/student/announcements01.png)

### Step 2
The whole message displays.
- If the announcement has a link in it, click the link to open the linked page.
- If the announcement has attachments in it, click the **Download** link to download the file.
- After you've read it, click the **Collapse** button to collapse the message.

![](/img/ulearning/student/announcements02.png)
