# Taking an Exam

### Step 1
From the course homepage, click the Exams tab.
![](/img/ulearning/student/exam01.png)

### Step 2
On the Exams screen:
- It lists all the exams assigned to you.
- Pay close attention to the Start/End dates of the exams and make sure you don't miss the due date.
- A progress status is assigned to each assignment.
- To take an exam, click the "Start exam" button for that exam.
![](/img/ulearning/student/exam02.png)

### Step 3
Click the "I know, now start" button.
![](/img/ulearning/student/exam03.png)

### Step 4
On the exam screen:
- At the top, it lists the exam information such as exam time, total possible points, total questions, exam availability, etc.
- A timer also shows at the top, indicating how much time is left for your exam.
- You have three choices for the exam you already started:
  - Take it next time
  - Save
  - Submit
- Pay close attention to how many sections the exam has and make sure you don't miss any of them.
![](/img/ulearning/student/exam04.png)

- Follow the instructions on the screen to complete each exam question.
- On the question number bar at the bottom, the questions with a dark background are the ones you have answered, while the ones with a light color background are the ones you haven't answered. You can click the question number to directly go to that question.
![](/img/ulearning/student/exam05.png)

### Step 5
To submit the exam, click the "Submit exam paper" button.
![](/img/ulearning/student/exam06.png)


### Step 6
Click the "Submit Exam" button on the popup window.
- Note: The message may inform you that you still have some unanswered questions. If you want to continue working on the exam, click the "Continue to answer" button.
![](/img/ulearning/student/exam07.png)
