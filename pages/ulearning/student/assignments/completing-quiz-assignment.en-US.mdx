# Completing Quiz Assignment

### Step 1
From the course homepage, click the Assignment tab.
![](/img/ulearning/student/assignment01.png)

### Step 2
On the assignment screen, it lists all the assignments the teacher assigned to the class:
- There are three types of assignments:
  - Individual assignment (marked with "Individual")
  - Group assignment (marked with "Group")
  - Quiz assignment (marked with "Quizzes")
- Pay close attention to the Start/End dates of the assignments and make sure you do not miss the due date.
- A progress status is also assigned to each assignment.
- To complete a quiz, click the "Write the homework" button for that quiz.

![](/img/ulearning/student/assignment02.png)

### Step 3
Follow the instructions on the screen to complete each quiz question.

![](/img/ulearning/student/assignment03.png)

### Step 4
After you've done, click the Submit assignment button.
![](/img/ulearning/student/assignment04.png)
