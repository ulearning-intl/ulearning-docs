# Creating Announcement 

### Step 1
On the course homepage screen, click the Create Announcement button.
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/announcement01.png)

### Step 2
From the Create Announcement popup window:
- Enter the announcement title.
- Enter the announcement content.
  - Note: It is a rich text editor field. You can enter texts, images, tables, links, etc. You can also upload attachments here.
- Click the Select Class link.

![](/img/ulearning/teacher/announcement02.png)

### Step 3

From the Select Class popup window:
- Select the class(es) you want to publish the announcement to.
- Click the Confirm button.

![](/img/ulearning/teacher/announcement03.png)

### Step 4 
From the Create Announcement popup window, click the Confirm button.

![](/img/ulearning/teacher/announcement04.png)

### Step 5 
You'll see that your newly created announcement is posted to the course announcement screen.

![](/img/ulearning/teacher/announcement05.png)
