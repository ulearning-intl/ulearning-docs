# Embedding a Youtube Video

### Step 1
- Click the **Add plugin** button.

![](/img/ulearning/teacher/prepare15.png)

- It pops up the **Insert plugin** window.

![](/img/ulearning/teacher/prepare16.png)


### Step 2
- Go to the YouTube video site and click the **SHARE** link underneath the video player.
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/prepare17.png)


### Step 3
- Click the **Embed** icon (the first icon in the Share popup window).

![](/img/ulearning/teacher/prepare18.png)


### Step 4
- Click the **Copy** link on the lower right corner of the **Embed Video** popup window.

![](/img/ulearning/teacher/prepare19.png)


### Step 5
- Go back to your Courseware Editor screen and paste the copied code into the **Insert plugin** popup window.

![](/img/ulearning/teacher/prepare20.png)

- Click the **Ok** button. The YouTube video is embedded into your content page.

![](/img/ulearning/teacher/prepare21.png)
