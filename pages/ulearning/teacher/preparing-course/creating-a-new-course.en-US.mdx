# Creating a New Course

1. Click the **Create Course** button.

![](/img/ulearning/teacher/prepare01.png)

2. - Enter the Course Name.
   - Select a Course Cover for your course.
   - Then, click the **Create** button.
   
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/prepare02.png)
The newly created course is listed as the first one in your Course List.

3. Click the newly created Course and it opens the course homepage.
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/prepare03.png)

<Callout type="info" emoji="ðŸ’¡">
  Notes: You may need to follow your school's course naming policy to give a name for your course.
</Callout>


**Helpful Tips**
- You can upload your own image as the course cover.
- Make sure the image is sized as 800px * 450px.
