# Previewing Courseware

From your courseware editing window, click the Preview button to view your courseware in editing as a student role.


![](/img/ulearning/teacher/prepare33.png)

It opens the content page in a new window. You can use the Previous Page and Next Page to navigate the whole courseware.

![](/img/ulearning/teacher/prepare34.png)
