# Creating Courseware

## Step	Action
1. Click the **Courseware** tab.

![](/img/ulearning/teacher/prepare04.png)

2. Click the **Edit/Create Course** link.

![](/img/ulearning/teacher/prepare05.png)

3. From your Courseware List screen, click the **Create Courseware** button.
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/prepare06.png)

4. - Enter the Courseware Name.
   - Then, click the **Create Courseware** button.

![](/img/ulearning/teacher/prepare07.png)

5. - To quickly create the content outline, just use the actual module, topic, and page titles to replace the placeholders and keep "#" signs intact.
   
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/prepare08.png)

   Here is an example: 

   Template placeholders | Example of actual module, topic, and page titles
   --- | ---
   #New Module | #Lecture 1
   ##New Topic | ##Topic 1: Karel Program
   ###Page 1 | ###An Algorithm vs Program
   ###Page 2 | ###Syntax of a Karel Program
   #New Module | #Lecture 2
   ##New Topic | ##Topic 1: Karel and Java
   ###Page 1 | ###Common Errors
   ###Page 2 | ###Decomposition

6. Click the **Create** button.

![](/img/ulearning/teacher/prepare09.png)

7. On the Courseware Editor screen:
   - The left area is the Structure pane where you can add/delete module/topic/page and reorder them.
   - The big middle area is the Content area where you can create the rich text content.
   - The right area is the Content Element tool bar where you can select a content element and insert it into the content area.

![](/img/ulearning/teacher/prepare10.png)

On the Structure pane, you can:
- Click the **New Module**, **New Topic**, or **New Page** to add a module/topic/page.
- Or click an item drop-down menu and then select the **Add Topic** or **Add Page** button for a corresponding item.
- From the drop-down menu, click the **Rename** button to edit the module, topic, or page title.


![](/img/ulearning/teacher/prepare11.png)

- Drag an item to reorder the content structure.

![](/img/ulearning/teacher/prepare12.png)
   
From the Content Element tool bar, you can select and insert 5 different content objects into the middle content area.

![](/img/ulearning/teacher/prepare13.png)
