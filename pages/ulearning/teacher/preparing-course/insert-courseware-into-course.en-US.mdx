# Inserting Courseware into Course

### Step 1

After you created, saved, and published your courseware, you can close the courseware page. Now you are back to your Course page at Courseware tab.
Click the Import My Courseware link.

![](/img/ulearning/teacher/prepare28.png)

###  Step 2
- Click the checkbox in front of the courseware you want to insert.
- Click the Import button at the bottom.

![](/img/ulearning/teacher/prepare29.png)

### Step 3

From the Modify courseware popup window, click the Confirm button.
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/prepare30.png)
