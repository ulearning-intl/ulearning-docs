# Creating Micro Course



### Step 1 
On the Resources screen:
- Click the Actions drop-down next to the resource you want to convert to a micro course.
- Click the Record Micro-course option.
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/resource07.png)

### Step 2 
It opens the Micro Course Editor window with the file loaded.
- Click the Record Voiceover button for the slide you want to add audio.
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/resource08.png)

### Step 3 
When you're done with the recording, click the Stop button to terminate the recording.
- Note: The recording time displays next to the Stop button.
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/resource09.png)

### Step 4 
Click the Play button to listen to your recording.
- If you are not satisfied, click the Clear Voiceover button and redo the recording.
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/resource10.png)

### Step 5 
If you want to clear the voiceover for this slide, click the Confirm button.
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/resource11.png)

### Step 6 
Click the next slide on the left slide thumbnail pane and record voiceover for it.
- Repeat this process to add voiceover to the slides you want to add voiceover to.
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/resource12.png)

### Step 7 
To add more slides to this micro course:
- Click the + sign at the top left corner of the screen.
- Select the file type you want to add and then click.
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/resource13.png)

### Step 8 
It opens the Add Resource popup window with all the available files of the type you chose, or you can upload a file from your local computer:
- Select the file(s).
- Click the Confirm (n) button.
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/resource14.png)

### Step 9 
Follow the same process to record voiceover for the newly added slide(s).
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/resource15.png)

### Step 10 
To adjust the order of the slides, drag the slide where you want it to go.
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/resource16.png)

### Step 11 
To remove a slide from your micro course, click the little Delete button on the top right corner of the slide thumbnail on the slide navigation pane.
- Note: Be cautious with this action since the deletion is irrecoverable.
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/resource17.png)

### Step 12 
Click the Preview button to preview the micro course in editing.
- Note: The micro course is automatically saved to the Resource Library. If you're not ready to publish this micro course, you're done with creating the micro course. You can close the Micro Course editor window and return to the Resource window.
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/resource18.png)

### Step 13 
The preview is the same as the view when your students are viewing this micro course. You can use the Up/Down button to go back/forward throughout the slides.
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/resource19.png)
