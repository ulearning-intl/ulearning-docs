# Publishing Micro Course

### Step 1 
If you are ready to publish this micro course in editing, click the Publish button.
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/resource20.png)

### Step 2 
Click the Confirm button.
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/resource21.png)

### Step 3 
The micro course is now listed on your course's Resources screen and is ready for the students to consume.
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/resource22.png)
