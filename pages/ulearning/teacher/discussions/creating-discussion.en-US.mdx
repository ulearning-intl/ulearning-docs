# Creating Discussions

### Step 1 
From the course homepage, click the Discussions tab.
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/discussion01.png)

### Step 2 
Click the + Discussion button.
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/discussion02.png)

### Step 3

- Enter the discussion title.
- Enter the discussion topic/instructions.
- Enter details or select options for all other items as desired.
- Click the Select Class button.
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/discussion03.png)

### Step 4 
On the Select Class page:
- Select the class(es) you want to assign this discussion to.
- Click the Confirm button.
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/discussion04.png)

### Step 5 
Click the Submit button at the bottom of the Create Discussion page.
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/discussion05.png)

### Step 6 
The discussion is now added to the Discussion screen.
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/discussion06.png)
