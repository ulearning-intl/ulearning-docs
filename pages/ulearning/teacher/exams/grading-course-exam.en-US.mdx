# Grading Course Exam

### Step 1 
From the Exams screen:
- Mouseover/Click the Actions drop-down menu for the course exam you want to grade.
- Click the View Results option.
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/exam21.png)

### Step 2 
On the exam's Exam Results screen:
- Locate the examinee/student you want to grade.
- Mouseover/Click the Actions drop-down menu for this student.
- Click the Grade Exam option.
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/exam22.png)

### Step 3 
On the Exam Grading page:
- The question serial numbers for the questions you can or have to manually grade are displayed at the top part of the screen. For example, Question 1, 2, 5, 6, 13, 14, 20, and 21.
- Locate these questions one by one on the screen.
- Review the student's answer and manually enter a score for each question.
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/exam23.png)

### Step 4 
To give customized feedback/comments to the grading you made for the question:
- Click the Add/Edit Comments link.
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/exam24.png)

- Enter your comments in the Teacher Comments field.
- Click the Complete link.
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/exam25.png)

- The comments are added to the grading for this question.
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/exam26.png)

### Step 5 
If there are multiple sections for this exam, make sure you navigate to all sections and grade all the test questions.
- Click the Save Grading button at the top part of the exam screen.
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/exam27.png)

### Step 6 
On the Exit popup window, click the Exit button.
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/exam28.png)

### Step 7 
On the Exam Results screen, the student's exam results are updated and refreshed:
- The Status is changed from Ungraded to Graded.
- The Manual Score/Total Score is changed from Pending to the actual scores. For example, 95/50.
- Total score is changed from Pending to the actual score.
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/exam29.png)

### Step 8 
To grade the exam for other students, repeat the steps.

Before grading, ensure you have selected the appropriate course exam and student to grade.
