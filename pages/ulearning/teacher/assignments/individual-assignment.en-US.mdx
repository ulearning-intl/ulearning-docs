# Creating and Publishing Individual Assignment

### Step 1 
From the course homepage, click the Assignment tab.
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/assignments01.png)

### Step 2 

- Click the + Assignment button.
- Click the Individual Assignment option.
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/assignments02.png)

### Step 3 

- Fill out the assignment details or select options as desired.
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/assignments03.png)

- Click the Select class button.
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/assignments04.png)

### Step 4 

- Select the class(es) you want to assign this assignment to.
- Click the Confirm button.
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/assignments05.png)

### Step 5 
The class(es) are added, and click the Publish button.
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/assignments06.png)

### Step 6 
The assignment is now added to the Assignments screen.
![](/img/ulearning/teacher/assignments07.png)
