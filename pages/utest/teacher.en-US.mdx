# Overview

This is a user guide for teachers. The aim of this guide is to give the ULearning teacher users the necessary knowledge and skills to use ULearning LMS’ exam feature – U-Test. By following the steps in this guide, you can quick start your journey to perform the most common U-Test tasks as a teacher.

This guide is organized around the necessary tasks you will perform, step by step accompanied by U-Test platform screenshots.
