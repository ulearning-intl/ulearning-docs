# Exporting Exam Score Report

To export the exam score report in ULearning, follow these steps:

### Step 1: Exporting the Exam Score Report

- On the Exam Results screen, click the drop-down menu.
- From the drop-down menu, select the "Export Scores" button.
![](/img/utest/teacher/result11.png)

The exam score report will be downloaded to your local computer. 
![](/img/utest/teacher/result12.png)

**Note:** Only the scores with the class/student information are exported in a Excel file.

By following these steps, you can easily export the exam score report from ULearning and access it on your local computer for further analysis and record-keeping.
