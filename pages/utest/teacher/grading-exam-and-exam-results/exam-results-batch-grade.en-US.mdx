# Batch Grade for Exam Results

To batch grade exams in ULearning, follow these steps:

### Step 1: Accessing the Batch Grade Screen

From the Exam Results screen, click the Batch Grade button.
![](/img/utest/teacher/result09.png)

It opens the Batch Grade screen.
![](/img/utest/teacher/result10.png)

- Use the left pane navigation links to move to next question [1].
- You can enter a score for student [2] and then click the Save button to save the score [3].
- Or you can click the Batch Grade button [4] to gave a same score to all students.
