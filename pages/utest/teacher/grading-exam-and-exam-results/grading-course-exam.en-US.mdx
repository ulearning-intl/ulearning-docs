# Grading a Course Exam

To grade a course exam in ULearning, follow these steps:

### Step 1: Accessing Exam Results

- From the Exams screen, mouseover or click the "Actions" drop-down menu for the course exam you want to grade.
- Click on the "View Results" option.
![](/img/utest/teacher/grading01.png)

### Step 2: Selecting a Student to Grade

- On the exam's Exam Results screen, locate the examinee/student you want to grade.
- Mouseover or click the "Actions" drop-down menu for that student.
- Click on the "Grade Exam" option.

![](/img/utest/teacher/grading02.png)

### Step 3: Grading the Exam

- On the Exam Grading page, the question serial numbers for the questions that require manual grading are displayed at the top of the screen (e.g., Question 1, 2, 5, 6, 13, 14, 20, and 21).
- Locate each question one by one on the screen.
- Review the student's answer and manually enter a score for each question.

![](/img/utest/teacher/grading03.png)

### Step 4: Adding Customized Feedback/Comments

- To provide customized feedback or comments for the grading you made on a question, click the "Add/Edit Comments" link.
![](/img/utest/teacher/grading04.png)
- Enter your comments in the Teacher Comments field.
- Click the "Complete" link.
![](/img/utest/teacher/grading05.png)
- The comment will be added to the grading for that question.
![](/img/utest/teacher/grading06.png)

### Step 5: Saving Grading and Exiting

- If there are multiple sections for the exam, make sure you navigate to all sections and grade all the test questions.
- Click the "Save Grading" button at the top of the exam screen.
![](/img/utest/teacher/grading07.png)

### Step 6: Exiting the Exam Grading

- On the Exit popup window, click the "Exit" button.
![](/img/utest/teacher/grading08.png)

### Step 7: Viewing Updated Exam Results

- On the Exam Results screen, the student's exam results will be updated and refreshed:
  - The Status will change from "Ungraded" to "Graded."
  - The Manual Score/Total Score will change from "Pending" to the actual scores (e.g., 95/50).
  - The Total score will change from "Pending" to the actual score.
![](/img/utest/teacher/grading09.png)

### Step 8: Grading for Other Students

- To grade the exam for other students, repeat the steps as needed.

By following these steps, you can effectively grade a course exam in ULearning. This process allows you to review student answers, assign scores, and provide customized feedback or comments. The exam results are updated accordingly, reflecting the grading status and scores for each student.
