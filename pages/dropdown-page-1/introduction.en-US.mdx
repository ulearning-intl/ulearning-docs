## I. Operation Path

【Members】-【Class/Student/Teaching Team Management】

## II. Operation Steps

Here you can manage the classes, students, and teaching teams associated with this course (course administrators can view all classes and students).

### 1. Teaching Team

Through 【Add Teacher】, you can add teachers who participate in teaching this course together. If it is a personal teaching mode, you don't need to add a teaching team.

Note: The associated teachers are personnel already created in the management backend. If you cannot find the teacher you want to add, please contact the school administrator.

Teacher Permissions: Courses can set three types of permissions: administrator, teacher, and teaching assistant; specific permission differences can be understood through 【View Teaching Permissions】.

Teacher and Class Management: You can use 【Assign Class】 to assign teachers to the classes they teach, and you can also use 【Remove from Course】 to unbind teachers from classes.

### 2. Class Management

#### (1) Create Class

**Method 1**: You can create through 【Class Management】-【Create Class】. When creating multiple classes at the same time, just enter them on new lines. You can also set whether approval is required when students join/leave the class, and whether students from other schools can join.

**Method 2**: Import from student database, import classes from other courses

#### (2) Class Approval

If approval is required for joining the class, you can approve in 【Class Join/Leave Applications】.

#### (3) More Operations

- **Student List**: Entry to 【Student Management】, click to jump to the corresponding list.
- **Add Student**: Associate school students to a class of a course.
- **Assign Teacher**: Associate school teachers to a class of a course.
- **Modify Class**: Modify class information.
- **Delete Class**: All students in the class will be removed from the course, and all activity records of students in the class will be cleared. Please proceed with caution.

### 3. Student Management

Students join the class independently: 【Members】-【Class Management】-【Class Code】, click to view the QR code.

The list shows all students under the course, and you can perform the following management operations:

- **Change Class**: Support changing students to other created classes of this course;
- **Remove from Course**: After removing a student from the course, the student's homework, discussions, exams and other records in the course will be deleted;
- **Reset Password**: Teachers can reset passwords for students. The reset password is a unified password. You can remind students to change the password after logging in;
- **Student Profile**: View students' course summary information and records of learning, homework, exams, etc.;
- **Export List**: Support exporting student list data.

### 4. Other Personnel Management

#### (1) Class Groups

Click 【Class Group Plan】 to divide class members into multiple groups, which can be used for 【Homework】-【Group Homework】.

#### (2) Face Authentication

Face authentication is used for student exams. If face recognition verification is enabled, you need to upload candidate authentication photos in advance. Photos can be imported in batches by teachers or uploaded by students themselves. Personal photos uploaded by students need to be reviewed by teachers, while student photos uploaded by teachers do not need review.
