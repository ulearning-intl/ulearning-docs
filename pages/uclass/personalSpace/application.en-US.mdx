# Frequently Used Applications

The Application Center provides a variety of teaching applications. Teachers can bookmark their commonly used applications, which will be displayed in the Personal Space after bookmarking. Click "Edit" to remove applications or rearrange their order.

![pic7](/Uclass_pics/en/pic7.png)

Clicking on an application allows direct access to the application within the client.
