# Participating in Activities

（1）Roll Call

After the teacher launches roll call, students will receive a notification in the app. On the roll call page, they can click "Check In Now" or scan the QR code displayed on the large screen to complete sign-in.

![pic53](/Uclass_pics/en/pic53.png)

(2) Pick  

The teacher randomly selects students on the large screen. The selected students then complete the classroom interaction as instructed by the teacher.

(3)  Q&A 

The teacher posts a question, and students can view it on their mobile phones and compete to answer. The student who grabs the opportunity can respond to the teacher's question during class.

![pic54](/Uclass_pics/en/pic54.png)

（4）Polls

Teacher initiates polls, after voting they can see the voting results.

![pic55](/Uclass_pics/en/pic55.png)

（5）Grading

Students or groups give presentations during class. The teacher initiates grading by setting the presenting student or group as the evaluation subject, and other students then rate their performance on their mobile devices.

![pic56](/Uclass_pics/en/pic56.png)

（6）Discussion

After the teacher posts a discussion, students can participate using their mobile phones. They can view posts from other students, reply to them, like them, and more. Through features such as class discussions, students can fully exchange ideas with their peers. Students earn points for participating in discussions and can view their own discussion activity records.

![pic57](/Uclass_pics/en/pic57.png)

（7）Upload a photo

In the classroom, especially for subjects like science, engineering, or English, many assignments still need to be presented using pen and paper in real time. Students can upload their handwritten work to the class space. The teacher can view all uploaded content from students and add annotations. After class, students can also view images uploaded by their peers, enabling cross-comparison of knowledge.

![pic58](/Uclass_pics/en/pic58.png)

（8）Quizzes

In the smart classroom, the teacher creates test questions to conduct in-class quizzes. Students use the mobile app to complete the quiz. After finishing, they can view their answer history and correctness feedback within the app.

![pic59](/Uclass_pics/en/pic59.png)
