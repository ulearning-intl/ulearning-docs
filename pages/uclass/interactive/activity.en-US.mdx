# Classroom Activities

（1）Pick

During the lecture, the teacher can randomly select or designate a student to answer questions. If roll call has already been conducted in the class, the teacher can select from the students who are present; absent students will not be included in the selection.

Random selection of students: Click the "Random Pick" button, and the system will randomly choose 1 student.

![pic26](/Uclass_pics/en/pic26.png)

Scoring student performance: Students will receive 2 points by default for participating in activities. The teacher can adjust the points based on the student's actual performance.

![pic27](/Uclass_pics/en/pic27.png)

（2）Q&A

Initiate Q&A activity: The teacher can enter the question/task in the input box (it is recommended to create a draft on the computer before class to save time during the lesson). Alternatively, the teacher can describe the question verbally to the students without entering text in the box. After selecting the number of respondents, click "Start", and students can then compete to answer on their mobile phones.

![pic28](/Uclass_pics/en/pic28.png)

Q&A: Students who successfully grab the opportunity can answer the teacher’s question. By default, students earn 2 points for participating in the Q&A activity, and the teacher can adjust the points based on the student’s performance.

![pic29](/Uclass_pics/en/pic29.png)

Student performance ranking: After the Q&A activity ends, students are ranked based on their performance.

![pic30](/Uclass_pics/en/pic30.png)

（3）Polls

Initiate a poll: The teacher fills in the poll topic and options (it is recommended to prepare a draft on the computer before class to save time during the lesson). Alternatively, the teacher can describe each option verbally without entering text. After clicking "Start ", students can vote on their mobile phones.
