# Present Teaching Resources such as PPTs and Videos

（1）Click the "Resources" button on the toolbar to open the course resource page. The teacher can select a previously uploaded PPT for presentation.

![pic20](/Uclass_pics/en/pic20.png)

(2) Click anywhere on the PPT page to advance to the next slide.  

(3) Use the arrow buttons on the right to go to the Previous or Next slide.  

(4) Click the button to open the PPT thumbnail view for slide navigation.

![pic21](/Uclass_pics/en/pic21.png)

Smart Classroom currently supports presentations of common documents (such as Word, PDF, Excel, and TXT files), videos, images, audio files, and graphic-text pages.
