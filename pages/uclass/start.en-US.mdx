# Smart Classroom Introduction

UClass3.0 Smart Classroom System focuses on the classroom teaching session and integrates the entire process of pre-class, in-class, and post-class across multiple dimensions such as teaching content, teaching interaction, teaching evaluation, and teaching data. UClass3.0 fully integrates and generates high-quality content resources, innovates teaching interaction modes, implements blended online and offline teaching, ensures teaching effectiveness, and enhances teaching efficiency.

![pic1](/Uclass_pics/en/pic1.png)

UClass3.0 includes Space, Classroom, Resources, and Application Center.

Space: The space displays tools commonly used by teachers, such as whiteboards, cloud drives, as well as frequently used teaching applications, making it convenient for teachers to quickly find the tools they need for teaching.
Classroom Interaction: Provides teachers with various teaching activities such as pick, quizzes, discussions, etc., to facilitate student participatory teaching.
Resources: Provides a variety of teaching resource libraries. Teachers can directly access resource libraries within UClass for teaching.
Application Center: Provides a variety of teaching applications. Teachers can bookmark their commonly used applications, which will be displayed in the Space after bookmarking.
