# App Center

The Application Center displays all the applications available at the school. Teachers can open the desired teaching applications during class for instructional purposes. The school's applications are configured by the school administrator in the management backend. If additional applications are needed, please request the school administrator to configure them.

![pic52](/Uclass_pics/en/pic52.png)

Teachers can click the "Favorite" icon to bookmark frequently used applications. Favorited applications will be displayed in their personal space for easy access next time.
