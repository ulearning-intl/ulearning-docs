# 1.1 How to switch to University-level/Department-level Administrator permissions when holding multiple roles?

Log in to the Lecture Capture and Video On-Demand Platform. By default, you are signed in as a Teacher. Use the menu in the upper-right corner to switch to the Administrator role.

![Review01](/Reviewers_pics/Review01.png)

After switching to the Administrator role, you can conduct online course inspections, add undergraduate teaching review tasks, and view course quality analysis reports.

In the “Live Stream” module, the Administrator can choose either Single Classroom View or Multiple-Stream View to access the management portal of “Recording & Webcasting”
