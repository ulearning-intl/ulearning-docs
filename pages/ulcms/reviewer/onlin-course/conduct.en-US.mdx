# 1.2 How to conduct an online course inspection by classroom, class schedule, or course?

In the “Lecture Review” module, select “Online Review.” The platform offers three options: “By Classroom,” “By Class Schedule,” and “By Course.” After selecting a class, you can view the live stream or playback recording to conduct your inspection.
