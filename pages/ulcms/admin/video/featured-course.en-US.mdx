# 3.2. How to Set Up the Featured Course Type, Subcategories, and Portal Display?

You can display designated courses and videos in “Special Recommendation” sections of the Video On-Demand Portal through the “Featured Course” Module.

![admin13](/Admins_pics/admin13.png)

**Create a Topic**

In the top-right corner of the topic list, click the “Create Topic” button. Enter the topic name and description, upload a thumbnail, and choose the content type to create the topic.

![admin14](/Admins_pics/admin14.png)

**Topic Content Management**

![admin15](/Admins_pics/admin15.png)

**Topic Categories:** You can add subcategory folders to group and manage courses or videos

**Add:** You can click to add courses, videos, and display them under a designated subcategory folder

![admin16](/Admins_pics/admin16.png)
