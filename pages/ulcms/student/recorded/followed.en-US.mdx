# 1.3 How to Set Up Followed Courses

In the **“Recorded Lecture”** module, students can go to **“Followed Courses”** to view the classes they have marked as favorites. If no courses have been selected yet, they can browse the **“Video On-Demand Portal”** to find and follow courses of interest.

![stu03](/Students_pics/stu03.png)

Students can click the **On-Demand Portal** button to enter, where they can browse and follow high-quality courses.

![stu04](/Students_pics/stu04.png)

After following a course, the selected courses will appear on the Lecture Capture and Video On-Demand Platform page.

![stu05](/Students_pics/stu05.png)
